Our Founder

Vibrant Lives Drive was founded in 1994 by Keneilwe Segwale, a dedicated oncologist passionate about making a difference in the lives of children diagnosed with cancer. As a young Oncologist Honors Graduate, Keneilwe recognized the gap in cancer care in her community and launched the organization to address these needs.

Our Story

What began as a mission to save lives and improve access to care in Alexandra, Gauteng, has grown into a worldwide movement supporting thousands of children and families. Keneilwe's unwavering dedication continues to inspire us all.
